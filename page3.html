Choose the Right Password

Instructions

When picking your password below keep these factors in mind.

  1. Length: Make your password at least 6 characters long.
  2. Complexity: Use a mix of uppercase letters, lowercase letters, numbers, and special characters (e.g., !, @, #, $, %, etc.).
  3. Avoid common words: Stay away from easily guessable words or phrases like "password," "123456," "qwerty," etc.
  4. Avoid personal information: Refrain from using personal information such as your name or birthday as it is easily obtainable.
  5. Substitutions: Replace letters with numbers or special characters that look similar. For example, "E" can become "3," "A" can become "@," and "S" can become "$."
